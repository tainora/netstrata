# Netstrata Celebrates Fifth Consecutive Win for Strata Management Business Of The Year Award!

**Date:** 2023-08-07T07:16:42+00:00
**Author:** S J
**Source:** https://netstrata.com.au/fifth-consecutive-win-strata-management-business-of-the-year-award/

---

In a milestone moment that underscores its relentless pursuit of excellence, Netstrata has clinched the Strata Community Management Business – Large at the Strata Community CHU Awards for Excellence Awards 2023. This prestigious achievement marks the fifth consecutive year that Netstrata has been recognised with this esteemed accolade, having previously won in 2019, 2020, 2021, and 2022.

The Strata Community CHU Awards for Excellence, organized by SCA (NSW), is a highlight of the NSW strata industry calendar. Each year, this awards program recognizes and honours industry leaders who have shown remarkable dedication, innovation, and success. Winning the award for the fifth time in a row not only reflects Netstrata’s continued growth but also affirms its reputation as an industry leader.

### A Journey of Excellence

Since its inception, Netstrata has consistently set new benchmarks in the strata industry. The consecutive wins since 2019 showcase a record of excellence that few can rival. This consistency stems from the company’s core philosophy of customer-centric service, dedication to quality, and an innovative approach that resonates with the ever-evolving needs of the strata community.

The 2023 awards ceremony was a glamorous affair, with over 700 individuals from the industry attending. Amongst a diverse and competitive group of finalists, Netstrata’s recognition spoke volumes about its relentless commitment to industry-leading practices.

### Gratitude and Team Spirit

In accepting this award, Netstrata’s leadership expressed profound gratitude towards SCA (NSW) for their tremendous efforts in hosting such a successful event. They also acknowledged and applauded their fellow finalists for their excellent contributions to the industry.

But the heartfelt thanks didn’t stop there. The company’s friends, family, clients, and colleagues were acknowledged for their continued support and trust. It was emphasized that this success is a shared triumph, and without the collective belief and hard work of all involved, Netstrata would not be where it is today.

### Building a Legacy

Five consecutive wins in this category is more than a mere accomplishment; it is a legacy. It showcases the commitment of Netstrata to not just meet industry standards but to continually set new ones. From building strong community relationships to implementing cutting-edge strategies, Netstrata’s dedication to excellence is clear in every aspect of its operations.

This latest win is not only a continuation of Netstrata’s tradition of excellence but also a promise of its future. The achievement is a beacon to other industry players and a testament to what can be achieved with unwavering dedication, strategic innovation, and a relentless drive for success, especially in setting the benchmark in professional strata management that benefits consumers.
