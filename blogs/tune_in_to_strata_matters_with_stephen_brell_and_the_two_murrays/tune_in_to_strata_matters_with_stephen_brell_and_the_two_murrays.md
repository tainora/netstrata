# Tune in to 2GB’s Strata Matters with Netstrata’s Stephen Brell and The Two Murrays

**Date:** 2023-11-14T05:38:22+00:00
**Author:** S J
**Source:** https://netstrata.com.au/tune-in-to-strata-matters-with-stephen-brell-and-the-two-murrays/

---

Join Stephen Brell, the accomplished Managing Director of Netstrata and concurrently, the President of the Strata Community Association, the peak industry body representing the strata sector. Engaging with the community on a broader scale, Stephen hosts ‘Strata Matters’ on Radio 2GB every Sunday afternoon, alongside the seasoned radio duo, Murray Wilton and Murray Olds.

With decades of collective and individual radio experience across prominent stations like 2GB, 2UE, and the ABC, the Two Murrays bring a wealth of knowledge and insight to the airwaves. Murray Wilton, also the General Manager of Agriculture and the Sydney Royal Easter Show for the Royal Agricultural Society of NSW, adds a unique perspective to the show.

Tune in to ‘Strata Matters’ for a dynamic radio experience where listeners can dial in, share their experiences in strata living, and gain valuable expert advice from Stephen. It’s an opportunity to stay informed, discuss relevant strata issues, and benefit from the collective wisdom of this seasoned radio team.
