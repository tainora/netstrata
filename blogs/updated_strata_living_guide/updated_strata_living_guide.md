### NSW Government’s Updated Strata Living Guide – Now Available

The NSW Government has released an updated version of the Strata Living – A Guide to the Essentials for Strata Owners, providing essential insights for current and prospective strata property owners.
This comprehensive guide helps you understand how strata works in NSW, outlining your rights and responsibilities, the laws you need to follow, and how to handle common strata-related issues.

#### What’s Covered in the Guide?

- The basics of strata ownership
- Common property vs. private property
- Key roles in managing a strata scheme
- Financial responsibilities
- Understanding by-laws
- Meetings and reporting requirements
- Building repairs and maintenance
- Renovation guidelines
- Dispute resolution

Whether you own or are considering buying a strata-titled property such as an apartment, villa, townhouse, duplex, or semi-detached house, this guide is an invaluable resource for navigating strata living.
