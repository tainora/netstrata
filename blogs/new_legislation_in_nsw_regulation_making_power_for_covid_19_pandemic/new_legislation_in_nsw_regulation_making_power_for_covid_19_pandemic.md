# New Legislation in NSW: Regulation-making power for COVID-19 Pandemic

**Date:** 2020-05-25T07:22:07+00:00
**Author:** S J
**Source:** https://netstrata.com.au/new-legislation-in-nsw-regulation-making-power-for-covid-19-pandemic/

---

Whilst keeping social distancing at the top of the agenda, the NSW Government has introduced a new Act for the purposes of responding to the public health emergency caused by the COVID-19 pandemic.

The new Act commenced on 14th May 2020 and covers topics such as convening strata meetings, voting, affixing the seal of the Owners Corporation and witnessing of same.
For a detailed description of the new Act, please visit NSW legislation:

https://www.legislation.nsw.gov.au/#/view/act/2015/50/whole#/part14/sec271a
