# Netstrata Wollongong Launch

**Date:** 2011-07-20T05:00:28+00:00
**Author:** S J
**Source:** https://netstrata.com.au/netstrata-wollongong-launch/

---

Last Friday evening the Team at Netstrata celebrated the opening of their Wollongong office at the Ibis Hotel in Market Street Wollongong directly opposite their new office. Licensee Tanya Gough said “It is fantastic to provide a strata alternative to the Illawarra”… A fantastic night was had by all.
