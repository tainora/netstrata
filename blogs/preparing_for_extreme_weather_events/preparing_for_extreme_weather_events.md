#### How to Prepare your Strata Property for Severe Weather events

With the Spring/Summer storm season on the horizon, there are a number of useful tips and tricks that you can undertake to protect your Strata Property and prepare (literally) for a rainy day.

Many Owners Corporations undertake routine preventative maintenance to prepare the common property for extreme weather such as regularly cleaning gutters and drains and having garage basement detention pit and pumps cleaned and serviced. However, the following guide has been prepared to assist individual strata residents to prepare their properties and ensure the safety and security of themselves and their loved ones during an extreme weather event.

We hope you find the following guide beneficial, and please remember that access to emergency trade services is available 24 hours a day by calling 1300 633 760.
